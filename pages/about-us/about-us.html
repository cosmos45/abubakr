About Us

Our Story

First established in 2001, Abu Bakr Supermarket was initially set up to provide the best, high-quality meat and poultry in the UK with its first supermarket at 37 Queen's Road, Burley, Leeds.

Following their success over the years, Abu Bakr Supermarket has since set up two more stores at 259 Dewsbury Road, Leeds and 215 Roundhay Road, Harehills, Leeds, with one more coming in 2021.

Abu Bakr Supermarket today are leading suppliers of retail and wholesale Halal products including poultry, fish, ethnic foods, fresh fruits and vegetables. We also provide other various groceries online such as household items. Abu Bakr also has established a very good reputation in the wholesale Halal foods sector.

Delivering Freshness Every Day

Our online delivery is a very popular service that covers the whole of Leeds. With the growing success of Abu Bakr Supermarket, we hope to deliver nationwide within the next few years.

We Take Sustainability Seriously

We're committed to reducing our environmental impact through sustainable packaging, reducing food waste, and supporting eco-friendly practices throughout our supply chain.

Supporting Local Products

We partner with local producers and suppliers to bring you the freshest products while supporting our community's economy and reducing our carbon footprint.

Our Services

Abu Bakr also now have Abu2Go, their hot food service which you can order straight to your door. This includes starters, main course dishes and sweets such as curries, pastries, biryani and mixed Mitai sweets.

Come on in and take a look if you're looking for Halal meat online or other wholesale products for delivery straight to your door.
